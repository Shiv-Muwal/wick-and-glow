Our Story

Born from a Flicker of Passion

Lumière began in a small Mumbai kitchen in 2019, when founder Kavya Nair couldn't find candles that were both truly natural and genuinely beautiful. She started making her own — slowly, intentionally, with love.

What started as a weekend hobby quickly grew into a movement. Word spread through social media, friends, and family. Each candle was different, each one carried a story. Today, Lumière serves over 12,000 customers across India.

Handcrafted candles made with natural soy wax and premium fragrances. Each candle is carefully designed to bring warmth, relaxation, and beauty to your home. We believe that the ritual of lighting a candle is a small but meaningful act of self-care.

What We Stand For

Our Values

🌿

Natural Ingredients

We source only 100% natural soy wax, pure essential oils, and cruelty-free fragrances. No synthetics, no shortcuts.

🤲

Small Batch Crafting

Every candle is poured by hand in small batches, ensuring quality, care, and individuality in every single piece.

♻️

Sustainability First

Recycled packaging, reusable jars, and carbon-conscious shipping. We're committed to leaving the planet better than we found it.

💝

Community Love

We partner with local artisans, support women-led cooperatives, and donate 1% of every sale to environmental causes.

🔬

Tested & Safe

All our fragrances are dermatologically tested and IFRA compliant. Safe for your home, safe for your family.

🎁

Gift-Ready Always

Every order is beautifully packaged and gift-ready. Because a Lumière candle should feel special from the moment you open the box.
